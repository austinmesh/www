When Austin Mesh holds meetup events we invariably end up with attendees that are new to the technology and do not have a radio or know where to start. We thought it would be a cool idea to have some pre-configured radios ready to go for handing out to newcomers.

[Heltec](https://heltec.org/) worked with us to procure some T114 devices that we can hand out at events. The T114 has proved to be a great handheld device and paired with our custom case makes for an easy way to onboard people to the mesh.

We felt this hardware paired nicely together to be a step above a typical "off-the-shelf" device:

- [Heltec T114](https://amzn.to/4wDAfJq) - A great low-power LoRa radio with OLED screen
- [Muzi Works Antenna](https://amzn.to/4dj3wS1) - Gizont comparable whip antenna, tested to have a good SWR
- [SMA to u.FL pigtail](https://amzn.to/3PKaBlD) - To connect the antenna to the T114
- [2Ah LiPo battery](https://amzn.to/4diZNnt) - Good for about a week between charges
- [Custom 3D printable case](https://www.printables.com/model/1709878-austec-heltec-t114-case) - Something we designed to be compact with recessed antenna

If you end up building one of these yourself we would love to hear about it! If you don't have a radio and are looking to get started then come to one of our meetups. You just might walk away with one.
